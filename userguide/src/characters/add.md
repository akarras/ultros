# Add Character

To use character-specific features, you first need to claim and verify your FFXIV character on Ultros.

1.  **Navigate to Profile**: Go to the [Profile](/profile) page.
2.  **Open Add Character Menu**: Click the **"Add Character"** button in the Characters section.
3.  **Search**: Enter your character's name in the search bar and click the search icon.
4.  **Claim**: Find your character in the search results and click the **"Claim"** button.
5.  **Verify**:
    -   Once claimed, the character will appear in the "Pending Verifications" list.
    -   Follow the instructions provided to verify ownership. This typically involves copying a verification code to your Lodestone profile bio.
    -   Once verified, the character will move to your main "Characters" list.
