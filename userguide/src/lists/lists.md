# Managing Lists

Lists in Ultros allow you to plan shopping trips, track crafting ingredients, or just keep an eye on item prices.

## Creating a List

1.  Navigate to the [Lists](/list) page.
2.  Click the **"Create List"** (+) button at the top right.
3.  **Name**: Enter a name for your list.
4.  **World/Region**: Select a Region, Datacenter, or World. This acts as the default filter for price checking items in this list.
5.  Click the **Save** icon to create the list.

## Managing Lists

On the main Lists page, you can see all your created lists.

-   **Edit**: Click the pencil icon to rename the list or change its region filter. Click the Save icon to confirm.
-   **Delete**: Click the trash icon to delete the list permanently.

## List View & Adding Items

Clicking on a list name takes you to the detailed view. Here you can add items and manage the contents.

### Adding Items

-   **Add Item**: Click "Add Item" to toggle the search bar. Search for an item, enter a quantity, and click "Add".
-   **Add Recipe**: Click "Add Recipe" to search for craftable items.
    -   **Craft count**: How many of the final item you want to make.
    -   **HQ Only**: Check this to require High Quality ingredients where applicable.
    -   **Ignore Crystals**: Check this if you don't want to add shards/crystals to the list.
    -   Click "Add Recipe" to add all necessary base ingredients to your list automatically.
-   **Make Place**: See the [Importing a Makeplace List](./import_makeplace.md) guide.

### Managing Items

The list table shows the item, quantity, HQ requirement, and a price viewer.

-   **Edit Item**: Click the pencil icon on a row to change the quantity or HQ requirement.
-   **Delete Item**: Click the trash icon on a row to remove it.
-   **Bulk Edit**: Click the "Bulk Edit" button at the top of the list.
    -   Select items using the checkboxes (or use "Select All").
    -   Click "DELETE" to remove all selected items at once.
    -   Click "Bulk Edit" again to exit this mode.

### List Summary

At the bottom of the list, you'll find a summary of the total estimated cost based on the cheapest listings found for your selected region.
